About the Artist

Striking hot iron since 2012.

The Man at the Anvil

I believe that metal has a memory. Every hammer strike, every heat cycle, leaves an imprint not just on the shape, but on the soul of the piece. My work is an exploration of this dialogue between force and flow.

Trained in traditional joinery and tool-making, I now focus on blending these ancient methods with organic, expressive forms. Whether it's a kitchen knife meant to be an heirloom or a gate that welcomes you home, each object is forged with intent.

Skills & Focus

  • Damascus Steel Forging
  • Traditional Joinery
  • Custom Architectural Design
